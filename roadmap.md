# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1 - MVP
- Upload two PDFs
- Extract text
- Compare content
- Show side-by-side PDFs
- Highlight differences
- Difference navigation panel

## Phase 2
- OCR for scanned PDFs
- Search within compared documents
- Better document structure extraction

## Phase 3
- Standards database
- Clause comparison
- AI review comments
- Compliance checking

## Phase 4
- Team collaboration
- Audit logs
- Version history
- Enterprise workflows
